When Structure Beats the Model

What Really Moves Markets When Theory Fails

This book explores how digital assets, decentralized protocols, and behavioral market dynamics are reshaping finance. It connects macro theory, crisis mechanics, and crypto infrastructure to explain how trust, code, and structure now govern value.
